Rubrics

Your GRE Analytical Writing task is evaluated based on specific criteria that measure how effectively you can express complex ideas and critique arguments. Two types of essays Issue and Argument are scored separately and then averaged for a final score between 0 and 6.

  • Clarity and Logic of Ideas: How clearly and logically you present your position or analysis.
  • Use of Reasoning & Evidence: The relevance and strength of examples, reasoning, and support for your claims.
  • Organization and Coherence: Clear essay structure, logical flow, and appropriate transitions between ideas.
  • Grammar and Vocabulary: Correct grammar and a varied, precise use of vocabulary to enhance clarity and style.
