Rubrics

Your IELTS essay will be scored based on four key criteria used by official IELTS examiners. Each area is rated from 0 to 9, and your final band score is the average of all four. This helps ensure fair, detailed, and structured feedback to improve your writing.

  • Task Response: How well your essay addresses the question and presents a clear, well-supported argument.
  • Coherence and Cohesion: The logical organization of ideas, clear structure, and appropriate use of linking words.
  • Lexical Resource: The range, accuracy, and flexibility of your vocabulary in context.
  • Grammatical Range and Accuracy: The variety and correctness of grammar and sentence structures.
